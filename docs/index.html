ABOUT

Thank you so much for visiting my website, I am so grateful for all the love and support.

I am a self-taught artist with no formal education in art. I was born in India and moved to England in 2013 to pursue my post-graduate studies in Engineering Management. After completing my studies and working for a while, I moved to Scotland with my husband and have been living in Scottish Highlands.

It is a great feeling to convert my passion into profession. I am recently concentrating on becoming a full-time artist and the love for colors & design is reflected in my work. My paintings are mostly inspired by people, places and experience that I have encountered through my travel around the world.

Most of the artwork fall under abstract category, contemporary modern painting, pen & ink drawing. Layers can be observed on the paintings at times to provide a unique sense of technicality through colors. All my paintings are hand painted and always one of a kind.
