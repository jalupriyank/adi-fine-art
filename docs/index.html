About

I am a self-taught artist , with no formal art education living in the Scottish Highlands. Originally hail from India and moved to the UK in the 2013 to pursue my graduate studies in Engineering Management. During this period, I quenched my desire to paint during my spare time and weekends.

My artwork is mostly inspired from colour, texture, movement and emotion. I mostly do mixed media artwork which primarily involves acrylic paint, watercolors and ink. It allows me to independently express the freedom of pouring paints onto a flat canvas or blank board. This spontaneous approach shows how simple the art is portrayed on through the medium but also to capture those elements perfectly of the subject such as the energy and the emotion.
